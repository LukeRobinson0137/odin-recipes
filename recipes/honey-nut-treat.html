Honey Nut Treat Recipe

Description

A beloved Skyrim snack, the Honey Nut Treat features plump, golden clusters of roasted nuts coated in sticky, glistening honey, skewered on a wooden stick, perfect for a quick bite on the road or by the hearth of an inn.

Ingredients

  • Pitted Dates
  • Golden Raisins
  • Sliced Almonds
  • Rolled Oats
  • Honey
  • Peanut Butter
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add dates and raisins to a food processor.
  2. Pulse until the mixture is smooth and no large pieces remain.
  3. Add almonds and oats to the food processor.
  4. Pulse a few more times until they are broken down and well combined with the fruit paste.
  5. In a separate bowl, gently heat the honey and peanut butter until warm and slightly runny.
  6. Add the processed fruit-nut-oat mixture to the bowl with the honey and peanut butter.
  7. Sprinkle in salt and cinnamon.
  8. Stir vigorously until everything is well-mixed and the texture is thick and sticky.
  9. Scoop out small portions of the mixture.
  10. Press the mixture between your hands to compact it, then roll into balls.
  11. Once all balls are rolled, gently thread them onto skewers.
