Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

I'm sure you also know how to make a grilled cheese but do you know how to properly use a toasty machine?

Stick around and let me tell you how to make toasted cheese and what could he mean by "know how to properly use a toasty machine"? Lets Find Out!!!!

Ingredients

  • Two Slices of Bread
  • Cheese (preferrably Cheddar Chesse)
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Butter the outsides of both slices of bread (this will be placed against the toaty machine).
  2. Grate some cheese.
  3. Spread some mayonnaise on the inside of the 2 slices of bread.
  4. Place the cheese on top of the first slice (must be sitting on mayo) and close with the second slice.
  5. Place in the toasty machine and switch on.
  6. Wait until the light turns green, your sandwich should be done.
  7. A lot of people switch the machine on and what till the light turns green before they place the sandwhich inside, they are wrong (my opinion) and I stand firm.
