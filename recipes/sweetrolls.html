Sweetrolls Recipe

Description

The Sweetroll is a soft, golden sponge cake with a moist, tender crumb, crowned with a thick layer of vanilla glaze that cascades down the sides like fresh snowfall, delivering a perfect bite of comfort and decadence—if you're lucky enough to get one before the guards do.

Ingredients

  • Warm Milk
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Dry Active Yeast
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cream Cheese
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Flour
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix warm milk and honey until the honey dissolves.
  3. In a bowl, mix warm milk and honey until the honey dissolves.
  4. Add the an egg, butter and flour to the bowl.
  5. Stir or mix until you have a smooth, lump-free batter.
  6. Spoon the batter evenly into a 5-inch miniature bundt pan (or multiple small molds).
  7. Cover the pan and allow the batter to rise for 30 minutes in a warm place.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until lightly golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. In a mixing bowl, beat together the cream cheese, butter, and powdered sugar until smooth.
  10. Gradually add heavy cream, a little at a time, until you have a thick, pourable icing that slowly runs off a spoon.
  11. Once baked, let the sweetrolls rest in the pan for 5 minutes.
  12. Then gently remove them and place on a wire rack to cool completely.
  13. Once fully cooled, spoon the icing over the tops of the rolls, allowing it to drizzle slightly down the sides.
