Burger Recipe

Description

I know you know how to make a burger. However, this burger is unlike any other burger you'd think it is. It's increadibly boring.

All you need is four ingredients, sorry no condemants with this burger, turn back now and find a better burger recipe.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Beef Pattie
  • Processed Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef pattie however you want, the wa you like it.
  2. Cut lettuce up into pieces and the burger bun in two halfves.
  3. Stack the lettuce first onto the bottom half of the burger bun.
  4. Followed by the beef pattie.
  5. Whip out that processed cheese and place it on top of the beef pattie.
  6. Close it off with the second/top half of the bun.
  7. Finally enjoy this incredibly boring burger.
